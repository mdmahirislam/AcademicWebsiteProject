Our Academic Programs

At DIMS, we offer a range of modern and career-focused programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in their chosen field. Below are some of our most popular programs:

Diploma of Information Technology

This program provides hands-on experience in programming, networking, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. Duration: 1 year full-time.

Certificate IV in Business Administration

Designed for future office managers and administrators, this course covers communication, project management, and business software. Duration: 6 months.

Certificate III in Health Services Assistance

This course prepares students to work in hospitals and aged care settings by focusing on patient support, infection control, and healthcare ethics. Duration: 6 months full-time.
